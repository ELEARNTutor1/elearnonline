About

Hi, my name is Shane. I’m a qualified adult education teacher. I built this website to develop my teaching experience and create helpful resources for students learning English as a foreign language. For now, I am letting it evolve naturally and structuring it as resources accumulate. I welcome any constructive feedback for improving my uploaded resources. You can reach me by email.

Methodology

My methodology is developing. Currently, I am student-centred, i.e., I make all efforts to place students needs and requests as a top priority. After all, it is their learning journey and experience. While students are sometimes shy to express their wishes or confront my approach, I encourage assertiveness and respectful confrontation. This is how I can improve my understanding of student preferences, evolving needs, likes and dislikes. As a teacher, I strive to deliver a learning structure based on constructive alignment, i.e. assessments are based on content taught and content taught is based on learning objectives. I also strive to deliver my lessons in an engaging way that facilitates learning. Following each assessment, I offer students mini certificates to highlight their ongoing achievements.

I am in the process of creating English language assessments based on Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) levels; A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, and C2, for listening, reading, speaking, and writing. However, this are currently under development. When complete, I will be better equipped to assess students’ needs and deliver level and goal appropriate lessons. Until then, I hope to support students on their language learning journey as best as I can!
